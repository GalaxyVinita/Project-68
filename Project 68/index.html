Campus Life

school library

The purpose of our school library is to support the educational work of our school. Everything in the library, and everything about the way it is organised, must be planned so as to help the children. Sometimes this help is given to the children directly, sometimes through the teachers.

school auditorium

The auditorium is a central and important space in any school lucky enough to have one. The acoustical design of an auditorium is different than that of any other school space, because of the specific demands of the performing arts, which distinguish the auditorium from a common lecture hall.

School Achievements

Inter School Debate

Today we had our School Debate contest and Elaine won the winner. The topic given was ‘Environment pollution’ and she was really excited, as it was a topic close to her heart. The judges were from ABC Technical College, and the BCD University. She started off well, since she was doing great in school. In the end, when the judges announce the winner, they were overjoyed! The judges commented that other students had fought well, Elaine had knowledge and fluency on her side!


Inter School Football Competition

11 international schools are competing in this fun and motivating event in the beautiful setting of the Santa Maria Polo fields. This event is one of the biggest international school events of its kind in Europe and the first time many young international students will have ever played competitive football against other schools. Girls and boys from 7 to 16 years old will be competing in 230 matches over three days.


Inter School Science Competition

Every year, more than 4 million students in grades 6-8 enter a science competition. Of those, only several thousand students in grade 8 participate in the topmost levels of competition.


What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The canteen provides the means by which children and adolescents can put into practice the nutrition messages they are being taught in the classroom. The canteen modeled healthier food choices that are tasty, interesting and affordable. This can influence food choices at school and in the wider community.

  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    “Online attendance management system” or “e-attendance” is one of them! Online Attendance Management System is an application developed for student's daily attendance in school. It helps to maintain the accurate record and generate summarized student attendance reports for the students by applying various criteria.
